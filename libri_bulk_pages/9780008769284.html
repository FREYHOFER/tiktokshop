Sophie Drearnever expected to fall in love. But over a summer in Maine, she does: with the dazzling flowers and the towering hedge maze of Lilymoor House.
But then, a door appears. Never in the same place twice. Leading only to a dishevelled garden, and a beguiling thundercloud of a man, confined inside.
Battling with the vines - just as much as she is with Lilymoor's owner's two inconveniently handsome nephews - Sophie knows that she is the only person who can help Lilymoor bloom again.
