Learning German for beginners can be fun – Langenscheidt's “Wie heißt das?” memory game makes it possible. The game contains 200 word and picture cards featuring the most important German words. Finding pairs of cards and learning terms – Memory is a game that everyone loves; it's universally understandable and fun. For each picture there is a matching card with the German word, and the terms are grouped thematically according to the most important everyday topics.• The fun way to learn German: The memory game contains 200 word and picture cards with the most important German words.• Find pairs of cards and learn terms: For each picture there is a matching card with the German word.• Topics relevant to everyday life: The memo cards are structured thematically, from the areas of living & household, family & friends, money & shopping, and others• Detailed game instructions: This easy-to-understand and simple game is explained in 4 languages (German, English, Arabic and Persian) and contains helpful tips for German lessons• German as a foreign language: With this memory game, the lessons can be made fun, providing quick and playful access to the language. Ideal for teachers of German as well as simply for everyday fun• Levels A1–A2: The language game is ideal for learning in tandem or in a group – for learners of all ages
