Über den Autor ▾▴
Lauren Palphreyman is an Amazon bestselling romantasy author. She likes to write strong and complicated men who respect women, women who can be soft as well as fierce, and romantasy tropes with a twist.
​​A proud indie author, she is best known for THE WOLF KING, which has been described as werewolves meets Outlander, has over 20k ratings on Amazon, and was recently acquired in a multibook audiobook deal with Simon & Schuster. Book two, THE NIGHT PRINCE is out now!
She got her start by writing serial fiction online, and you might also know her from her mythological paranormal series, CUPID'S MATCH, which was one of the top stories on Wattpad, garnered over 70 million 'reads' online, and was adapted into a digital pilot for The CW (unfortunately we didn't get the full series!).
A Yorkshire girl at heart, she now lives in Surrey with her partner and their mischievous cockapoo - who steals far too much of her attention (and socks) while she's trying to write!
