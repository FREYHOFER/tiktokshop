Florida show jumper Hallie Oakley was raised in the saddle. Now she's the rising star on Team USA.
And she has just one goal in mind - an Olympic gold medal. But to achieve this, she must train with the most prestigious equestrian facility in the world.
Nestled in the English countryside, Golden Oaks might look like nothing more than a beautiful stables, but it has produced some of the sport's greatest riders. . . including Hallie's idol, Jasper Knighton.
The brooding oldest son in a showjumping dynasty, Jasper has recently inherited Golden Oaks and is determined to defend its legacy.
From the moment Jasper and Hallie meet, their attraction is instant and mutual.
But when a shocking event threatens to tear Golden Oaks apart, Jasper knows he'll do whatever it takes to protect what matters most to him.
Because losing Golden Oaks would be devastating - but losing Hallie is something Jasper refuses to risk.
Über den Autor ▾▴
Tillie Cole hails from a small town in the North-East of England. When she is not writing, Tillie enjoys nothing more than spending time with her little family, curling up on her couch watching movies, drinking far too much coffee, and convincing herself that she really doesn't need that last square of chocolate.
