Into the unknown: Join the Illumineers on their journey through Lorcana.
Following the victory over Ursula, the realm throws a party, but a cryptic map is found amid the revelry. It hints at a hidden island across the seas. The Illumineers set sails and discover powerful ink magic. But as they test their new skills, a villainous vizier is about to rise to power.
This second Collector's Guide tells the story behind the game and includes an overview of all cards from sets 5-8 - the perfect addition to the Lorcana TCG for collectors, players, and Disney fans!
Disney Lorcana is a popular trading card game in which players embark on exciting quests with their favorite Disney characters. But it's more than just a game: stories await around every corner in the magical world of Lorcana. Here, Luminari use magical ink to summon countless glimmers of popular Disney characters, locations, and items, and embark on quests to protect and preserve the stories of Lorcana. The official Collector's Guides to the Disney Lorcana Trading Card Game combine these stories with exciting Easter eggs and an overview of all cards from a given year. The second volume contains the stories and a card catalog from these sets: "Shimmering Skies," "Azurite Sea," "Archazia's Island," "Reign of Jafar," and "Illumineer's Quest: Palace Heist."
