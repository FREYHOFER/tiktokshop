Awake or dreaming?
For the past six months, Loren Calla's life has consisted of one simple objective: stay alive.
With the help of her new friends and the love of a ruthless Darkslayer, she has defied the odds. But with the Veil rapidly falling, and swarms of bloodthirsty creatures invading Yveswich, survival has once again stepped beyond her reach.
Pushed into a war between worlds, she must ask herself a question: what?or who?is she willing to sacrifice for victory? For love?
Darien Cassel fears that his luck is running out. Trapped deep underground, his Sight rendered ineffective by the darkness of another realm, it seems things can't get any worse.
But when a new threat emerges, he is forced to push himself to the breaking point?and this time, if he falls, he may not get back up.
Roman Devlin's heart has been stolen by a beautiful thief. His father has demanded that he stay away from Shayla Cousens, but when their paths reconnect by force of circumstance, Roman finds himself falling for Shay all over again.
With enemies closing in from all sides, Roman is faced with decisions that have the potential to either shape his future into what he always dreamed it could be...or destroy it.
In this breathtaking fourth installment in the House of Devils series, all must fight if they wish to stand a chance against the dark forces poised to devour their world. But when tragedy strikes again, they realize that nothing, not even love. may be enough to save them this time.

Über den Autor ▾▴
Kayla Edwards is the author of the House of Devils series - City of Gods and Monsters, City of Souls and Sinners, City of Lies and Legends, and City of Smoke and Brimstone. She is also the author of the upper-YA romantasy novel, Dreams of Ice and Iron. She started writing City of Gods and Monsters when she was in high school, so the characters and the world they live in are very close to her heart. When she isn't writing, she enjoys traveling, spending time in nature, and binge-watching her favourite television shows with her husband. You can connect with her in her Facebook readers group Dare's Devils, or follow her on Instagram, Booktok, and Pinterest @kaylaedwardsauthor
