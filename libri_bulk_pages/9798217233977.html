#1 NEW YORK TIMES BESTSELLER • This Little Golden Book, based on Netflix's hit animated film KPop Demon Hunters, is sure to delight HUNTR/X fans of all ages!
Rumi, Mira, and Zoey are pop stars by day and Hunters by night! Meet them along with Saja Boys, the hottest new demon-boy band on the scene, Derpy Tiger, Sussie Bird, and more beloved characters from KPop Demon Hunters in this Little Golden Book.
