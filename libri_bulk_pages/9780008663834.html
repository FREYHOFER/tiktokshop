When their thirteen-year-old daughter Barbara goes missing at summer camp, all eyes fall on the Van Laar family.
They're cold, rich people. They own the camp. And it's happened to them before.
Fourteen years ago their little boy, Bear, vanished without a trace.
Two children lost to the same wilderness.
Is it a tragedy... or a crime?

Über den Autor ▾▴
Liz Moore is the author of five novels, most recently the New York Times-bestselling The God of the Woods, which was a Barack Obama pick and the winner of the Jimmy Fallon summer book club. She is also the author of Long Bright River, also a bestseller, which has been produced for TV by Peacock and will release in 2025. Moore's novels have been translated into more than twenty-five languages. The winner of a 2014 Rome Prize in Literature, Moore lives with her family in Philadelphia.
