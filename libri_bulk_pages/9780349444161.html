Über den Autor ▾▴
Elsie Silver is a no. 1 Sunday Times and New York Times author of sassy, sexy, small town romance who loves a good book boyfriend and the strong heroines who bring them to their knees.
She lives just outside of Vancouver, British Columbia with her husband, son, and three dogs and has been voraciously reading romance books since before she was probably supposed to.
She loves cooking and trying new foods, traveling, and spending time with her boys-especially outdoors. Elsie has also become a big fan of her quiet five am mornings, which is when most of her writing happens. It's during this time that she can sip a cup of hot coffee and dream up a fictional world full of romantic stories to share with her readers.
