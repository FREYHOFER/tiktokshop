Welcome to Lorcana, a magical world of imagination!
After arriving in Lorcana, the Illumineers learn how to summon glimmers. Driven by curiosity, one of them unleashes a flood of magical ink. It carries precious treasures with it into the Inklands. In their search for them, the Illumineers encounter a great threat.
This first Collector's Guide tells the story behind the game and includes an overview of all cards from sets 1 to 4 - the perfect addition to the Lorcana TCG for collectors, players, and Disney fans!
Disney Lorcana is a popular trading card game in which players embark on exciting quests with their favorite Disney characters. But it's more than just a game: stories await around every corner in the magical world of Lorcana. Here, Illumineers use magical ink to summon countless glimmers of beloved Disney characters, locations, and items, and embark on quests to protect and preserve the stories of Lorcana. The official Collector's Guides to the Disney Lorcana Trading Card Game combine these stories with exciting Easter eggs and an overview of all cards from a given year.
The first volume contains the stories and a card catalog for these sets: "The First Chapter," "Rise of the Floodborn," "The Inklands," "Ursula's Return," and "Deep Trouble - an Illumineer's Quest".
More info at www.disneylorcana.com
