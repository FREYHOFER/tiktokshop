I accidentally witnessed a brutal murder. So to protect myself, I pretended I saw nothing.
But my plan backfired. And now I'm the target of cold-blooded revenge.
Jude Callahan isn't just a hockey god-he's a devil no one dares to cross.
He's also determined to make me pay for my moment of silence.
Now, no matter how much I try to hide from him, Jude finds me.
I thought he'd stop at the stalking.
Or even better, kill me and finally end my misery.
But Jude has other plans.
He says I can't die. That I must pay for my sins.
And just like that, he drags me into his depraved world, kicking and screaming.
