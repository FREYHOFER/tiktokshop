Klappentext ▾▴
Skate into the world of Puckboys, the hot mm gay ice hockey romance fiction series for fans of Heated Rivalry and Emily Rath.
Cody needs a wingman to stop him marrying the first woman that comes onto him and Miles is just the man for the job.
Cody Bilson is leaving his four ex-wives and Seattle behind for a fresh start in Nashville. There, he joins a new team alongside Miles Olsen, a rookie goalie starting his first professional season.
Cody finds Miles cocky and annoying, but he is soon charmed by the younger goalie's quirky personality - and his handmade friendship bracelets. The media is quick to ship this new bromance, eager to see what kind of trouble these seemingly straight best friends will get into. Cody, who is notorious for falling in love too fast, relies on Miles to keep him on the straight and narrow... or perhaps just the narrow.
As their mutual dry spell becomes too frustrating, and completely oblivious to their deepening feelings for each other, Miles proposes an idea Cody can't refuse, why not blow off steam together?
The friends to lovers mmc romance fans of Rachel Reid and Emily Rath didn't know they needed.

Über den Autor ▾▴
Eden Finley is a bestselling author who writes steamy contemporary romances that are full of snark and light-hearted fluff. She doesn't take anything too seriously and lives to create an escape from real life for her readers. She's also an Australian girl from Queensland and apologises for her Australianisms that sometimes don't make sense to anyone else.
Saxon James is a Top 10 International Bestseller from Australia who writes MM romance to make you smile. Whether you love sports romance or small town, college guys or men getting their second shot at love, Saxon has something for everyone.
