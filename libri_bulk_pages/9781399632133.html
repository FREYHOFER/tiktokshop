'Lingers long after the last page' REBECCA ROSS, Sunday Times bestselling author of DIVINE RIVALS
THE DARK AND DELICIOUS CONCLUSION TO THE BOOKTOK SMASH HIT!
****
Leucosia has gained a new queen... and a new monster.
King Nemea is dead by Imogen's own hand, leaving Eusia with one less devoted servant-but the deity's strength has only grown. Now, she can access Imogen's power freely, and what's worse, Imogen's first taste of Eusia's grotesque magic has left her hungry for more.
Meanwhile, Theodore's wedding to Halla is imminent, and Imogen plans to steal his bride and use her to get to the blighted island where Eusia's body resides. But the journey proves more difficult and the waters more treacherous than she could have imagined, tempting Imogen to descend into her magic's darkness. As she fights to balance her feelings for the handsome king with her mission, and draws nearer to severing her bond with Eusia, Imogen realizes that ending Eusia might cost her more than she had ever planned on giving.

Über den Autor ▾▴
Kalie Cassidy was born and raised in Southern California and spent over a decade working in LA as a professional theater actor, coach, and acting teacher. She now lives in Cleveland, Ohio, with her two children and husband. She spends most of her time dreaming up new stories in her library, gardening, walking the nearby woods, and learning about "real" weather.
