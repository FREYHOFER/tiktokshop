Klappentext ▾▴
For the first time ever, the entire #1 New York Times bestselling Red Queen series is captured together in one box set. This stunning package features all four blockbuster Red Queen novels—Red Queen, Glass Sword, King’s Cage, and War Storm—as well as the short story collection Broken Throne.
This limited edition, 10th anniversary collection is finished with floral edges dripping in red and silver blood, creating a seamless, striking design across all five volumes that will look stunning on fans’ bookshelves.

Power is a dangerous game.
Mare Barrow, a lowly Red in a world of red-blooded laborers and silver-blooded elites, thought she knew what her future held. Until she learns that her red blood has been disguising a secret ability—one usually reserved only for Silvers.
The Silver ruling class is determined to keep her secret hidden from the increasingly restless Red population and their Scarlet Guard revolutionaries. But will they succeed?
Or will Mare use her forbidden power to change the caste system, the kingdom, and the world—forever?
In the dark and dangerous world of Red Queen, no one is as they appear, and the only certainty is betrayal. Follow the series from the spark that began everything—all the way to the electrifying conclusion, and beyond. 
Über den Autor ▾▴
Victoria Aveyard is the #1 New York Times Bestselling author of the RED QUEEN series and the REALM BREAKER trilogy. Her books have been translated into over 40 languages and counting. Born and raised in a small town in Western Massachusetts, she now lives in Los Angeles with her dog and her husband.
