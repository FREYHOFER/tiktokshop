Darcy Mooreis tired of death. Which would be fine...if she wasn't the Grim Reaper.
Gabriel Aetos is determined to protect his little corner of the world like his angel wings depend on it.
When Darcy shows up to Gabriel's faded old bar, she is hell-bent on outrunning her reputation as a villain. She won't be dragging her next assignment to the underworld. She'll be saving him instead.
But Gabriel is convinced that Darcy is as deadly as it gets. And if protecting his people means staying close - very close - to this rogue reaper, then that's exactly what he'll do.
Only, magic never comes without consequences...and these two spirits may just be forced to reap what they sow.Featuring all your favourite tropes:
