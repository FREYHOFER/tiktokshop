HE CAN'T HAVE HER ... BUT HE'S TAKING HER ANYWAY

Bridget von Ascheburg only wants three things: to feel true love, to experience fiery passion, and - most importantly - to be able to make her own decisions. Yet she is bound by her status as princess of Eldorra, especially when her brother suddenly refuses to take the throne. Bridget is forced into a position she never wanted. But she is not on her own: Rhys Larsen, her stoic bodyguard, is always by her side. Bridget and Rhys, however, feel much more for each other than they dare to admit. Revealing their true feelings could endanger the entire kingdom, and even cause its ultimate downfall ...

Über den Autor ▾▴
Ana Huang is an author of primarily steamy New Adult and contemporary romance. Her stories range from light-hearted to dark, but they all have HEAs with plenty of banter and swoon sprinkled in. Besides reading and writing, Ana loves travelling, is obsessed with hot chocolate, and has multiple relationships with fictional boyfriends.
