The Academy of Dream Analysis: A highly addictive enemies to lovers romantasy series 
Those who dream, sin. 
Those who don't, perish
Nemesis von Winther comes from a family of dreamwalkers. She is sent to study at the remote Academy of Dream Analysis in northern Finland, where she is to be trained as a lucid-someone who can shape reality through their dreams. At the Academy, she meets Mercy, the headmistress's enigmatic nephew. In the landscape of his dreams, Nemesis loses herself-and her heart. Yet no one suspects that she is at the Academy to avenge her brother's death, for which she believes the headmistress is responsible. But Mercy, of all people, stands in her way... 

Dark and dreamy: Your next obsession!
