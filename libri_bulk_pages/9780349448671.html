He had her, but then he lost her.
Malachi Vize has always had one desire in life. One goal. One deep obsession he could never give up on. His foster sister, Olivia.
For the first time, Olivia has chosen him over everything else she holds dear, and Malachi doesn't intend to ever let her go. But his happiness is soon shattered by powerful enemies who want to claim Olivia for themselves.
Wounded and wrestling with his own demons, Malachi is forced into unexpected alliances to hunt them down, but he won't stop fighting until he gets her back.
No matter what it takes.
Little Liar is a dark, taboo romance and contains explicit sexual content and difficult themes. Please read the content warnings before reading. Recommended 18+.
