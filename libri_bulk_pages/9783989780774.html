In this #1-Spiegel-bestselling seasonal romcom fake dating was the plan-catching real feelings wasn't.
Becca and Nils are best friends - who end up in bed together every New Year's Eve. On the 10th anniversary of their annual one-night stand, kindergarten teacher Becca scrapes together her savings, planning to finally confess her love to Nils at a romantic Alpine chalet. But things don't go as planned. When Nils shows up with his new fiancée, Becca asks Raphael, the obnoxiously snooty uncle of one of her students, to play the role of her fake boyfriend. The two had always clashed but, when sparks fly, the fire between them is anything but fake...
