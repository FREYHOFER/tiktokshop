Skate into the world of Puckboys, the hot mm gay ice hockey romance fiction series for fans of Heated Rivalry and Emily Rath.
They're as different as fire and ice. But that doesn't mean the sparks won't fly...
Aleksander is newly divorced, recently out as pansexual, and ready to celebrate singledom. What better way than to party with the Collective, a supportive group of LGBTQ+ hockey players?
When firefighter Gabe is called to a party gone awry, it's business as usual. Until he literally collides with drunk, smoking hot NHL megastar Aleks. Gabe has zero interest in hockey, but could be tempted to tune in if every player looks like Aleks.
There is no doubt about the heat between them, which is about the only thing the pair agree on. The new unlikely friends strike a deal - Gabe will help Aleks navigate casual dating, no strings attached. But when Gabe starts catching feelings, he struggles not to rush Aleks into something he's not ready for.
Hilarious and sexy, this friends with benefits romance will be the new obsession for fans of Rachel Reid.
