*Sunday Times Bestseller*
The fourth spellbinding book in the Interactive Illustrated Harry Potter series, brought to life in spectacular full colour by Karl James Mountford, featuring eight magical, moving paper-engineered elements. Prepare to be enchanted!
This gorgeous, interactive edition of Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire presents the wizarding world in every dazzling detail! Harry's adventures at Hogwarts have been richly reimagined in vibrant, gem-bright colours, from the hustle and bustle of the Quidditch World Cup to the silvery wonder of the Yule Ball. Exquisitely made and visually breathtaking, this special edition looks amazing on any bookshelf - a gift for Harry Potter fans of all ages.
In addition to J.K. Rowling's complete, unabridged text and full-colour illustrations on nearly every page, there are also eight iconic paper-engineered moments to enjoy. Readers can pull a name from the flaming Goblet of Fire, watch the Dark Mark appear in the night sky and follow Harry into the lake during the Triwizard Tournament.
Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire is an edition to collect and treasure, and an enchanting way to share this beloved series with a new generation of readers.
