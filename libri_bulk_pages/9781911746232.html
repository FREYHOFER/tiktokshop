Edgy. Dangerous. Addictive. From the Sunday Times bestselling author of the Silver Pines series comes a binge-worthy dark motorcycle romance, perfect for fans of Rina Kent and Navessa Allen.
Aiden Foxx runs the Disciples of Sin motorcycle club with strict control and zero tolerance for weakness. A former Navy SEAL turned outlaw king, he lives by one rule - trust no one.
Then Mia Tyler walks into his bar. Platinum hair, ocean eyes, and a body that tempts him to break every vow he's ever made.
Foxx knows Mia is dangerous. He knows she's hiding something. But when suspicion collides with desire, resisting her becomes impossible, even after he discovers she's a rival with her own deadly agenda.
When war ignites, Foxx must decide: is she the enemy who could destroy him. . .or the only woman who might save him?
