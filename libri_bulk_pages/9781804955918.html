When Cecily Knight sneaks into the Heathen Club's initiation on behalf of a rival gang, she's confident she can slip through the night unnoticed.
But the moment she steps into their den, she knows she's made a fatal error. The devil has seen her.
Jeremy Volkov may not technically be the devil, but as far as Cecily's concerned, he's close enough.
He's not just a member of the Heathens-he's their ruthless leader and the heir to the Russian Bratva.
And now, Jeremy wants one thing: Cecily.
He claims it's to protect her, but Cecily knows better. He wants to possess her, own her, make her his.
But Cecily is not about to be dragged into his blood-soaked world... or is she?
