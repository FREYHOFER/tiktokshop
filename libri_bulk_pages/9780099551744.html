**DETECTIVE HOLE IS NOW A MAJOR NETFLIX SERIES**
When the snow falls, the killing begins.
After the first snowfall, a mother vanishes, leaving behind only a pink scarf wrapped around a snowman's neck. Detective Harry Hole recognises the signature - a serial killer who hunts when the world turns white.
As the body count rises, Hole's obsession deepens. His pursuit of the 'Snowman' draws him into a web of betrayal, fear and unimaginable cruelty.
Atmospheric, ingenious and bone-chilling, The Snowman is Nesbo at his most ruthless.
