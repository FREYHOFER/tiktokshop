Klappentext ▾▴
¿You are cordially invited to a most enchantingLondon Season...¿
The Season's most eligible bachelor is Lord Blake, Viscount of Thorwood. Classically handsome and obscenely rich. The unmarried ladies are sure to swoon at his feet.
One must not forget Mr Carstairs, he may not be titled but he is most certainly rich and handsome in his own right. One might call him the underdog of the Season.
An unexpected interloper by the name of Miss Violet Bertram has appeared for the first time this year. A recent heiress with a most mysterious and magical aura.
The Viscount and Mr Carstairs seem to be completely spellbound by Miss Bertram. One might even say the depth of affections this young heiress has inspired feels almost like an enchantment...
Will the Season end with charmed suitors waiting at the altar or is scandalous magical mischief afoot?
Über den Autor ▾▴
Natasha writes books about magic, secrets, and adventure for adults and children. The Frost Fair, her debut children's novel, was longlisted for the Branford Boase Award. She studied History at Cambridge. How To Charm A Viscount is Natasha's debut novel for adults.
