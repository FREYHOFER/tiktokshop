PRE ORDER THE SIZZLING ENEMIES-TO-LOVERS ROMANTASY
👑 The desert doesn't forgive-and neither does she.
In a world where magic means death, a woman hiding her power hires a brooding gunslinger to guide her across a dangerous wasteland in this slow-burn, high-heat romantasy where trust is a liability, survival isn't guaranteed, and the monsters aren't the greatest threat.
Perfect for fans of ACOTAR and The Gunslinger, this sizzling, enemies-to-lovers romantasy is a bullet-laced ride through a magic-starved kingdom.
