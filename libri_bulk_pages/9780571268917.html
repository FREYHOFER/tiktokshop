Paperback edition of the debut novel from a young Nigerian author, which is shortlisted for the Dylan Thomas Prize. A modern day "Romeo & Juliet", set in Lagos.
Über den Autor ▾▴
Chibundu Onuzo was born in Lagos, Nigeria in 1991. Her first novel, The Spider King's Daughter, won a Betty Trask Award, was shortlisted for the Dylan Thomas Prize and the Commonwealth Book Prize, and was longlisted for the Desmond Elliott Prize and the Etisalat Prize for Literature. She is completing a PhD on the West African Students' Union at King's College London.
