A book of stories haunted by a rumor: a play so captivating it unravels the mind.

In The King in Yellow, Robert W. Chambers moves from Parisian bohemia to shadowed streets and sickly twilight horizons, where beauty and terror share the same frame.

With its iconic imagery and uncanny atmosphere, the collection stands at the origin point of an entire tradition of modern horror and strange fiction.

This is the English-language paperback edition of the classic book, following the 1895 text.
