Klappentext ▾▴
From the author of the Terminate the Other World! series: An unlikely dungeon master must create an awesome apiary army before his enemies smoke them out.
As a kind-hearted beekeeper, young Belissar was never one to boast about anything. But after he was rescued by a beneficial bee core and gifted a tower, life has certainly exceeded his wildest expectations. In fact, he's become a serious contender in a world in which the unworthy very quickly become the unalived.
And that's the ending a whole juggernaut of strong, nigh-eternal, highborn tower lords have in mind for Belissar. They're on the way, and they want him annihilated-along with everything he's ever created. But he isn't about to run. He's come too far, worked too hard, and sacrificed too much to abandon his hives, friends, and tower.
Instead, Belissar puts his mind and minions on total war footing (or buzzing). They're not only developing monster bees that can heal, harm, or unleash hell but also training with his insects and allies to fight as one. Meanwhile, Belissar's increasing his own power to a point that makes even him a bit nervous.
Unfortunately, he's still massively outnumbered, outmatched, and soon to be out of luck. But what Belissar doesn't know is that he's not alone. He's being watched by long-hidden eyes from another realm that find him quite . . . interesting. And they may hold the key to not just the hive's survival but the future of their world.
The fourth volume of the hit LitRPG fantasy series-with more than four million views on Royal Road-now available in paperback, ebook, and audiobook!
Tropes include: unlikely hero, anthropomorphic animals, hive mind, natural leader, and unstoppable enemy.
